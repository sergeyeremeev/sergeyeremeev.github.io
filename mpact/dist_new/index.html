MPACTDIGITAL.COM - HELPING CHANGE THE FACE OF DIGITAL ADVERTISING…
INTEGRATE YOUR LIVE TV, CABLE AND SATELLITE FEEDS ON TV SCREENS SHOWING ADVERTISEMENTS THROUGHOUT OUR DIGITAL SIGNAGE NETWORK. 

We utilize strategically placed displays to reach consumers with highly targeted messages in venues that include transportation hubs such as airports; executive networks in office-building lobbies and elevators; networks in condominium buildings, shopping malls, fitness centers, medical clinics and other locations. This provides a great marketing medium for Advertisers to target potential clients/consumers!
Advertisers can customize their messages to a specific location, time of day, or special event to increase the return on their marketing campaigns. The TV screens in our network are located in high traffic areas which have custom static, semi-static and fully moving commercials/advertisements for various local and national businesses.

Host Locations

Our digital signage advertising will not interrupt your cable/satellite TV feeds! Instead, we will be able to overlay the advertisements on your screen around the cable/satellite TV program so your audience will continue to enjoy watching the TV show while being exposed to the advertisements on the side and bottom of the screen. If you want to generate additional revenue on existing TV screens AT NO COST to you in your buildings or wish to discuss installing new/additional TV screens, email or call one of our knowledgeable host representatives to discuss further.

Account Executives

Digital Advertising

MpactDigital.com provides local business owners with a way to advertise their products and services digitally to the public. We focus on providing information by using our LED screens which are placed in high traffic areas such as condos, local businesses, in commercial buildings and airport lounges. At this time we are in search of motivated inside/outside sales representatives in the South Miami and Fort Lauderdale areas.

This position is responsible for selling digital advertising space in our network, and ensuring client satisfaction. Account Executives will create results-driven media solutions for clients looking to target a niche market through digital advertising.

YOUR TYPICAL DAY WOULD INCLUDE:

  • Prospecting potential retailers and service-based companies by phone and in person
  • Soliciting and developing new business accounts
  • Selling digital advertising space in multiple buildings
  • Overseeing the collection of ad material
  • Managing client issues
  • Developing client relationships with exceptional customer service
  • Maintain positive relationships with internal staff as well as to work cooperatively within a team environment

If you are interested in either of the above positions, please apply via email to eileen@mpactdigital.com with a current copy of your resume & a list of 2 work references (contact name, position, company, phone number and email address) of people you have directly reported to in the past. Please, no other colleagues or personal references.  Resumes will be reviewed as they come in and we will follow up with candidates that would be a good fit with this opportunity.
